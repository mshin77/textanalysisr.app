Named Entity Recognition

NER identifies named entities in text. spaCy's English models use OntoNotes 5 with 18 entity types.

People & Groups

  • PERSON – Person
  • ORG – Organization
  • NORP – Nationality, Religious, or Political Group (American, Buddhist, Republican)

Places

  • GPE – Geo-Political Entity: countries, cities, states
  • LOC – Location: non-GPE locations (mountains, rivers, regions)
  • FAC – Facility: buildings, airports, highways, bridges

Time

  • DATE – Date: absolute or relative dates and periods
  • TIME – Time: times smaller than a day
  • EVENT – Event: named hurricanes, battles, wars, sports events

Quantities

  • MONEY – Monetary Value
  • PERCENT – Percentage
  • QUANTITY – Quantity: measurements of weight, distance, etc.
  • CARDINAL – Cardinal Number: numerals not in other categories
  • ORDINAL – Ordinal Number: first, second, third

Works & Products

  • PRODUCT – Product: objects, vehicles, foods (not services)
  • WORK_OF_ART – Work of Art: titles of books, songs, etc.
  • LAW – Law: named documents made into laws
  • LANGUAGE – Language
